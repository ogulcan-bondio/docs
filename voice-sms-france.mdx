On Bondio, you can now sell voice & SMS enabled eSIMs with French local phone numbers to your customers without any extra effort on your side. All you need to do is:

1. Create a voice plan
2. Have voice eSIMs in your inventory

Then, with the same APIs, you can provision and sell voice-enabled eSIMs to your customers.

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is included?">
    - Unlimited incoming and outgoing voice calls within France to regular (non-premium) phone numbers
    - Unlimited incoming and outgoing SMS within France to regular (non-premium) phone numbers
    - Data as configured with the plan
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is not included?">
    - International (outside France) calls & SMS, both incoming and outgoing, are not allowed
    - VoLTE, VoWiFi, MMS, Visual Voicemail, and SMS A2P are not supported
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the cost?">
    - One-time cost: $0.50 / eSIM
    - Monthly active cost: as given in your account
    - Data: per-GB price as given in your account
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create a voice plan?">
    1. Go to the plans page and click **New plan**
    2. Enter `name`, `data` & `validity` as per your requirements. For `coverage`, choose **France (voice)** as the plan coverage
    3. Click **Create Plan**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to do a KYC (Know Your Customer) check?">
    No, KYC is not required if the voice eSIM is used for 30 days or less. In case of any further usage, the reseller is responsible for doing the KYC.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I order voice eSIMs?">
    1. Go to the eSIM order page
    2. Click **New Order**
    3. Select the quantity for **Chi eSIM (Voice Data SMS)**
    4. Click **Pay Now**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the delivery timeline of voice eSIMs?">
    In the worst case, it can take up to 5 working days to deliver voice-enabled eSIMs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
